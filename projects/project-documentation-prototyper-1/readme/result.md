# Result: [LockQuest]

All components have been successfully integrated and work together synchronously. The hardware is housed in a custom-built chest that ensures both protection and ease of use.

The combination of sensory detection, acoustic feedback and mechanical interaction provides a novel and engaging experience. The successful overcoming of technical challenges, such as the integration of the gyro sensor and the synchronization of the sound module and servo, underlines the effectiveness of our development process.

We are proud of the current status of the project and look forward to presenting “LockQuest” in the upcoming presentation.
