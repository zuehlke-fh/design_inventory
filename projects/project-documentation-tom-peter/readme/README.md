## Basic Topic Description
This project focuses on creating an electronic MIDI controller in the form of a trumpet. The aim is to capture the playing experience and expressive capabilities of a traditional trumpet and translate them into MIDI signals to control virtual instruments or synthesizers.

Key features:
Valve Control: Buttons or sensors for MIDI note input.
Breath Control: A sensor to capture airflow and control dynamics like volume.
Pitch Modulation: Pitch bend or slide functions using additional sensors.
USB MIDI: Direct connection to DAWs without extra hardware.

The goal is to develop a versatile and expressive controller for music production and live performances.

## Idea and Inspiration
The idea for this project came from wanting to combine the expressive feel of playing a real trumpet with the flexibility of digital music. The trumpet’s dynamic sound and unique playing style make it a great fit for controlling virtual instruments in creative ways.

The main motivation was the desire to create a MIDI controller that offers more expressive possibilities than traditional key-based controllers. The choice of a trumpet shape was primarily for fun.

The goal was to keep the natural feel of the trumpet while opening up new possibilities for sound design and performance.
