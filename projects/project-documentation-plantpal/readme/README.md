## Basic Topic Description
The project „PlantPal “ aims to create an intelligent, automated plant irrigation system to develop that is suitable for use in households as well as in urban horticultural projects such as vertical farming. The system continuously monitors the moisture content of the soil of the plants using sensors. Based on the recorded data of the individual sensors, „PlantPal “ controls the irrigation of the plants efficiently and as required

## Idea and Inspiration
The idea for "PlantPal" was inspired by the challenges many people face in maintaining healthy plants, particularly in urban environments or busy lifestyles. Plants play a vital role in improving well-being and air quality, but improper watering and insufficient light often lead to frustration and plant loss.

"PlantPal" aims to simplify plant care by automatically monitoring soil moisture and light levels, ensuring plants receive the right amount of water and light when needed. Additionally, the project draws inspiration from Vertical Farming, which addresses urban space limitations and promotes sustainable food production. By integrating "PlantPal" with Vertical Farming, the goal is to create green urban spaces that provide fresh, locally produced food while reducing environmental impact.
